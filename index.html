How we playtest

  • Design a non-generic poll to verify specific aspects of the game
  • Playtest until statistically significant results are obtained
  • Understand and discuss the results
  • Come up with least invasive & most creative design improvements
  • Define the expected effects
  • Repeat till the desired outcome is achieved
